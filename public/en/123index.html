Chronicle of Forgotten Times: Pawn of the Gods - This is a fabulous journey across various locations where you will face many dangers on your way. Fight various and dangerous enemies using your cunning and skills in battle. The game is inspired by the Zelda BOTW games and the Dark Souls series.

In the story, you play as the legendary knight Daire, who by the will of fate, almost single-handedly fought against the rebellious feudal lord Riogall, who started a civil war in order to overthrow the current royal dynasty and become the king of Ayderia.
